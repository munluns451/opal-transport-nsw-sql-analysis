# Australian Retail Sales Analysis 🇦🇺

## Project Overview
Analysis of 500+ Australian retail stores across 5 cities using SQL and SQLite.
The goal was to identify top performing cities, business types, and customer behaviour patterns.

## Tools Used
- SQL (SQLite)
- DB Browser for SQLite
- Python (pandas) for data conversion

## Dataset
- 500 rows of Australian retail data
- 5 cities: Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, Adelaide
- 6 business types: Clothing, Grocery, Electronics, Furniture, Bookstore

## Business Questions & Key Findings
1. Sydney has the highest average daily sales at $3,068
2. Clothing is the top business type by weekly revenue
3. Debit Card is the most popular payment method
4. Clothing has the highest customer satisfaction with 79 Good ratings
5. Sydney and Perth are tied with the most stores at 130 each
6. Clothing is the most profitable business type

## Key Insight
Clothing is the standout performer in Australian retail — highest revenue,
highest satisfaction, and most profitable. Sydney leads all cities in daily
sales despite being tied with Perth on store count.
